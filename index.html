Word Shift

WordShift is an app to increase or decrease the character count of a paragraph. This is great to reduce tweets or to pump up your essays. Not all suggestions will fit your needs so make sure to double check for grammar!

Step 1: Type or paste your text into the textbox below

  • Words: 0

  • Characters: 0

Step 2: Toggle arrow up/down to maximize/minimize character count, then hit optimize!
